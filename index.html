Grammar Reference · Modal Auxiliaries

Modal auxiliaries are helping verbs that express necessity, permission, obligation, ability, or possibility. When writing rules or requirements, modals give your sentences the right level of authority.

Use them in the pattern: Subject + Modal + Base Verb  (e.g. "Students must submit their work on time.")

must must not should should not have to may may not can cannot could will would shall need to ought to
